# Product Route Pages

Future internal route pages should deepen the four public routes without changing the main landing conversion path.

## Routes

- `/technique`
- `/client-flow`
- `/independence`
- `/mentorship`

## Required Page Structure

Each route page must include:

- pain;
- symptoms;
- what it fixes;
- product substance;
- first step;
- CTA to Telegram;
- future paid product.

## Route Notes

`/technique` should focus on precision, speed, clean results, materials control, architecture, and quality.

`/client-flow` should focus on the path from visibility to booking: packaging, offer, price framing, messaging, objections, and response logic.

`/independence` should focus on transition from employment or another person's system into a stable professional trajectory.

`/mentorship` should focus on diagnosis, personal correction, implementation control, and guided route work.

## Guardrails

- Keep each route diagnostic-first.
- Do not invent fake metrics, testimonials, or cases.
- Do not turn route pages into generic course pages.
- Keep Telegram as the first conversion step until product details are verified.
